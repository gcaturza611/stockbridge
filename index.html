Welcome to Stockbridge American International School

Dare to lead. Be a Global Business Leader.

The Stockbridge American International School (SAIS) is the first and only International School in Davao and General Santos Cities, Philippines offering early childhood (6 months old to 5 years old) and basic education (Kinder to Grade 12) programs. SAIS prides itself as a truly international school that provides internationally-benchmarked curricula and innovative learning experiences that develop students to become business leaders with global competencies.
